Event Information

July 7th through July 9th

FireFly Festival is a three-day music festival featuring a diverse lineup of musicians from various genres, including rock, pop, indie, and electronic music. The festival is geared towards music lovers of all ages who are looking to experience great live performances and discover new artists. The festival's theme is inspired by nature and the outdoors, with a focus on creating a welcoming and inclusive atmosphere for all attendees.Catch us at The Oak Tree Farm in New York!
